Project Introduction

A black body is an idealized physical object that perfectly absorbs all incident electromagnetic radiation. This simulator demonstrates how thermal radiation spectra depend solely on temperature. The "Ultraviolet Catastrophe" is illustrated by comparing the classical Rayleigh-Jeans Law (red dashed line) which fails at short wavelengths, with Planck's Law (solid white line) which founded quantum mechanics.
