💡 The Vision

Physics doesn't have to be static. For our Physics-I Microproject, we wanted to bridge the gap between complex quantum theory and interactive web design. Lux-Planck is a real-time simulation that allows you to play with the laws of the universe—specifically how light and heat interact in a perfect vacuum.

"If you want to find the secrets of the universe, think in terms of energy, frequency, and vibration."

🛠️ Built With

HTML5 - The skeleton of our universe.

CSS3 - For that dark-mode, glassmorphism aesthetic.

JavaScript - Powering the Planckian math engine.

Chart.js - Bringing the spectral curves to life.
